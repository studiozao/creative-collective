What you can be part of

A free programme for creative businesses right across East Sussex, delivered by Studio Zao with East Sussex County Council. Start wherever suits you.

Hacks

Open to everyone · 2 hours · free

Free, 2-hour working sessions, and the easiest way to get started. Real challenges, tangible takeaways, and a room full of peers rather than a lecture. Sessions cover pricing, audience building, hiring, AI tools and accessing grants, and run across East Sussex in Lewes, Hastings and Eastbourne.

Mentoring Sprints

By invitation · 6 hours, one-to-one

Six hours of one-to-one time with a mentor who has built a creative business themselves. A short diagnostic call comes first to find your focus, then structured sessions built entirely around what you need.

The Collective

Free to join · ongoing

Open to any actively trading creative business in East Sussex. Meet other creatives, get support that actually fits, and stay part of the network long after the programme ends.

What happens after your first Hack

Every Hack is open to anyone, free, and the easiest way in. You’ll meet other creative businesses across East Sussex, pick up something practical, and see whether the deeper support is right for you. No pressure either way.

1

Get invited to mentoring

Once you’ve been to a Hack, or simply told us you’re interested, you’re invited to apply for the mentoring scheme.

2

Go through the diagnostic

A short survey, then a 30-minute call to talk through your situation and agree the focus for your mentoring Sprint.

3

Start your Sprint

You’re matched with a mentor and begin your 6-hour Sprint, at whatever pace suits your business: a few short weekly sessions, or a couple of longer deep-dives.

4

Stay part of the Collective

Once your Sprint ends, you’re an alum: you’ll get a short summary of next steps, keep your invite to future Hacks, and, if it’s a fit, come back later as a mentor yourself.

Places on the mentoring Sprint are limited and matched to fit, so we can’t guarantee mentoring for everyone who registers. Every business is welcome at the Hacks and the wider Collective regardless.

And through all of it, you’re part of something bigger: a community of creative businesses across East Sussex, at the Hacks, in the mentoring, and beyond. You’re not doing this on your own.

Is this for you?

  • Actively trading creative business based in East Sussex
  • Working in design, digital, craft, film, music, publishing or a related creative field
  • Sole trader, freelancer or small business

Not sure if you fit? Come along to a Hack or get in touch, and we’ll help you work it out.
