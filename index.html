Three ways in, one community

A free programme delivered by Studio Zao in partnership with East Sussex County Council, running for creative businesses right across East Sussex.

Hacks

Free, 2-hour practical working sessions — the main way to get involved. Real challenges, tangible takeaways, and a room full of peers, not a lecture. Covering pricing, audience building, hiring, AI and digital tools, and accessing grants. Held at locations across East Sussex, including Lewes, Hastings and Eastbourne, with a few short webinars and briefings alongside to explain the offer and introduce the mentor pool.

Mentoring Sprints

6 hours of one-to-one support with a mentor who's built a creative business themselves. Starts with a diagnostic conversation to find your focus, then structured sessions built around it.

The Collective

Free to join, open to any actively trading creative business across East Sussex. Meet other creatives, get support that actually fits, and stay part of the network long after the programme ends.

Start at a Hack, go as deep as you need

Every Hack is open to anyone, free, and the easiest way in. You'll meet other creative businesses across East Sussex, pick up something practical, and see if the deeper support is right for you — no pressure either way.

  1. 1

    Get invited to mentoring

    Once you've attended a Hack, or told us you're interested, you're invited to enrol in the mentoring scheme.

  2. 2

    Go through the diagnostic

    A short survey, then a 30-minute call to talk through your situation and define the focus for your mentoring Sprint.

  3. 3

    Start your Sprint

    You're matched with a mentor and begin your 6-hour mentoring Sprint, built around your specific focus.

And through all of it, you're part of something bigger — a community of creative businesses across East Sussex, at the Hacks, in the mentoring, and beyond. You're not doing this on your own.

Places on the mentoring Sprint are limited and matched to fit, so we can't guarantee mentoring for everyone who registers. Every business is welcome at the Hacks and the wider Collective regardless.

Is this for you?

  • Actively trading creative business based in East Sussex
  • Working in design, digital, craft, film, music, publishing or a related creative field
  • Sole trader, freelancer or small business

Not sure if you fit? Come along to a Hack or get in touch — we'll help you work it out.

Real people, not a call centre — you'll meet the same faces throughout.
